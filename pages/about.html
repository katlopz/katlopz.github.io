About

Kathy is a singer-songwriter, composer and coder based in Wellington, New Zealand. A recent graduate from Victoria University of Wellington, studying computer science and music…

She was a finalist in the Living Room Songwriter’s Competition 2020 with her original song Treehouse Memories.

Kathy is currently the musical director of Filcoro, the Wellington Filipino Community Choir. She debuted her first choral arrangement, Lead Me Lord, with the group.

She still enjoys teaching piano in her free time and has free piano teaching resources available here.
