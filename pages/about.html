About

I am a Wellington based coder interested in software and game development. Particularly exploring how in-game actions can directly impact the composition of music; ensuring that every playthrough is a new sonic experience.

In the compositional field, I am currently working on a singer-songwriter debut album titled 31, a collection of songs each written on consecutive days in January 2021. I hope to work on one song each day in months that exclusively have 31 days.
